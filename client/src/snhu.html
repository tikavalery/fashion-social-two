2024 SNHU-a-polooza

Home

November 9, 2024 Berinyny Valery

The city of Woodstock, NY is excited to introduce a new monthly event series, SNHU-a-palooza! Taking place on the first Friday of each month at Woodstock farm, the events will feature live performances from musicians and bands, as well as visual artists including painters and others. These events will be spread across multiple stages and areas, with specific locations detailed on the event pages. While admission is free, attendance will be capped at 5,000 people. To attend, individuals must register on this website for each event they wish to participate in. Attendees must bring either a printed event confirmation or a valid ID for entry.
